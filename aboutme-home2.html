Meet Paul Myers

Paul is a junior at New York University in its Tandon School of Engineering. He is studying Integrated Digital Media, and beyond being a content creator, he has an interest in researching applications of virtual reality to improve health as well and an interest in pro-health and LGBTQQIA-inclusive game design.

He is a gamer, a musician, a birdwatcher, a brother, and a twin.
