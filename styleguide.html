Font and Font Sizes

Three fonts are used on this website: Ailerons, Coves, and Kano. Links to the three free fonts are provided in the stylesheet for this style guide. Fonts are used in specific areas to strike a proper balance of branding and legibility. All text should be centered, and the fonts and the sizes are the same on mobile as they are on desktop. They are as follows:

Aeilerons

  • Header 64px
  • Page Title 48px

Aeilerons is also used in the icon artwork on the homepage.

Kano

  • Footer 16px

Coves

  • Navigation Bar 16px
  • Captions 24px

On screen-widths up to 800px, the page background should be the lightest tone of that page category's specific hue. If the page is 800px wide or wider, the lightest tone of the "home" color should be used. This is because neutral grays prevent detracting from the content itself, and that is the most light, neutral tone I have to offer.

On screen-widths up to 800px, the header and footer are solid colors. On screen widths wider than or including 800px, the header and footer have an alpha value of 0.8 assigned.

Naming and filing Conventions

All pages that showcase content need to be in their specific folders. Any page that does not explicitly showcase content should have the shortcut name assigned to it. Attachments, extra files, and stylesheets should all use their category's shortcut name in their name, and it should be in its specific folder. Instead of using camelCase, spaces, or underscores, different parts of the file name should be separated using hyphens

Shortcut names include:

  • About Me: aboutme
  • Photo/Art: art
  • Audio: audio
  • Design: design
  • Game Design: games
  • Streaming: stream

The first page seen in each category, as well as the home page, are accessible directly in the parent folder. Child folders for each section exist, and in those folders, child pages should be made. Child folders for styles and images exist as well, and within those are folders to separate different types of content
