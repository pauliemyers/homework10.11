The Cyoub: A Storage Solution

The Cyoub is a prototype of a home solution designed for an Ideation and Prototyping class assignment. It is a storage cube designed to create usable space by being modable, stackable, and hangable without disturbing clothes on hangers.

When a higher fidelity prototype is created, this page will be updated with pictures.
